Consultation

Your consultation includes a full clinical assessment, referral for digital scans and a panoramic X-ray, and a detailed evaluation of your teeth and bite.

Based on this, we create a personalised treatment plan outlining the most suitable options, expected timeline, overall approach, and cost.

The consultation fee is fully credited towards your treatment if you choose to proceed.

Braces

We use high-quality, clinically proven materials to ensure precise and reliable tooth movement throughout your treatment.

Braces treatment typically starts from around EGP 40,000 for metal braces, depending on the complexity of the case and the duration required.

An initial payment of 25% of the total fee is required to begin your treatment. The remaining balance is then divided into monthly payments, typically from around EGP 2000, depending on the overall plan and treatment duration.

Treatment usually lasts between 12 and 24 months, although more complex cases may take up to 30 months.

Ceramic braces are also available as a more discreet option and involve an additional cost.

Appointments are generally scheduled every 4 to 6 weeks to monitor progress and make the necessary adjustments.

Aligners

Clear aligners are a preferred option for those looking for a more discreet and comfortable treatment.

They are most suitable for mild to moderate cases and are designed to fit easily into your daily routine.

Treatment includes digital scanning and a series of custom-made aligners, planned step by step to gradually move your teeth into position.

Costs typically start from around EGP 50,000, depending on the complexity of the case.

An initial payment of 25% of the total fee is required to begin your treatment. The remaining balance is then divided into monthly payments, typically from around EGP 2500, depending on the overall plan and treatment duration.

Treatment duration is usually between 12 and 18 months.

Aftercare

After your treatment is complete, we provide the retainers required to keep your teeth in their new position.

We continue to monitor your progress and ensure everything remains stable over time.

Your plan includes two follow-up visits during the first year after completing treatment, allowing us to check alignment and make any necessary adjustments.

Ongoing guidance is provided to help you maintain your results long term.
